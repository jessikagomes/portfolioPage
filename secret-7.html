About

Client: Secret 7''
Work: Vinyl Cover Art
My role: Concept, Art Direction, Illustration

Secret 7'' is an annual event where seven tracks from seven musicians are illustrated by several graphic artists around the world, which will result in a one-of-kind sleeve for a seven inches vinyl.

You could only find out which song was on the vinyl after the purchase. The covers were displayed in an exhibition and the money was donated to the social cause Art Against Knives.
