About

Client: Coca-Cola
Work: Burger Code
My role: Concept, Art Direction
Agency: Ogilvy

On Burger Day,Coca-Cola invited consumers to transform their burgers into a powerful solidarity message on social media. In partnership with Ogilvy and Just Burger, located in São Paulo, Brazil, the brand supported an initiative where the burgers’ bread had an especial printed QR code.

After the QR code was scanned, consumers were directed to For All The Tables campaign content, where orders that included Coca-Cola in several delivery establishments were converted into donations to “Ação da Cidadania”. A link was also provided where people could donate directly to the NGO.
